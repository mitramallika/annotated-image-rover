Louis Marshall School’s Community Garden

Sebert Harper, a horticulturalist and environmental educator, teaches a lesson at Louis Marshall School’s community garden in Canarsie, Brooklyn.

“The kids are the future. I want them to have the knowledge of where their food comes from.” – Sebert Harper

Teachers at Louis Marshall School accompany the students during the gardening classes.

>

Students at the Louis Marshall School learn how to maintain a garden and incorporate healthy foods into their meals.

“They’re more health aware.” – Daniel Redomdo, the parent of a fourth-grader.

Some of the students had never been to a garden before their school developed one.
